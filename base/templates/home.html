About E-Summit

Over the years, our flagship event, E-Summit, has hosted several prominent stalwarts in the industry and is the second largest Summit of its kind in eastern India. Featuring entrepreneurship guest talks, workshops, competitions, and investor meet-ups, E-Summit aims to build a strong startup ecosystem by exposing students to entrepreneurship.
